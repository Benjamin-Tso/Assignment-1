Section 1 - Design
Q1. What do you consider a ‘state’ in this environment? How would you define a ‘neighboring state’ for this problem?
    I will consider a state in this environment to be some board state at any point, a neighboring state would be one with one 
    more or fewer shapes
Q2. First-choice local search requires that you move to a random neighboring state. How
do you (plan to) implement random neighbor selection? Explain in 1-2 sentences.
    a random neighbor selection would be done by randomly adding or removing a brush stroke, the added brush stroke's position and color will be random
Q3. Specify your objective function. Are all constraints modeled through the objective
function, or are some treated as explicit rules? Explain your choices.
    Because the goal is to use 1. as few colors, 2. as few shapes, 3. no adjacent cells matching colors, and 4. fill the board
    the objective function will be a product of a measure of the 3, in some variation of
    a(# of colors) + b(# of shapes) + c(# of empty cells)
    where the goal is to minimize
